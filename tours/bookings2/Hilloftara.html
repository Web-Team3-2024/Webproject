    • Hill of Tara

    Discover the Hill of Tara, one of Ireland's most iconic ancient sites, for just €20. Located in County Meath, this historic landmark offers a unique journey through Irish mythology and history. Once the seat of Ireland's High Kings, the Hill of Tara features fascinating earthworks, including the Mound of the Hostages and the Lia Fáil (Stone of Destiny). Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside and learn about its rich heritage through guided tours. Perfect for history enthusiasts and families alike, the Hill of Tara promises an unforgettable experience, blending culture, nature, and a glimpse into Ireland's past.

Nestled in County Meath, Trim is a town rich in history, culture, and stunning landscapes. Home to the iconic Trim Castle, Ireland's largest Anglo-Norman fortress, it offers a unique blend of medieval heritage and modern charm. Stroll through its picturesque streets, explore the scenic River Boyne, and uncover stories in every stone. Whether you're drawn to historic andmarks or cozy Irish pubs, Trim promises an unforgettable experience.

Where to go?

Trim Castle

Discover the awe-inspiring Trim Castle, Ireland's largest Anglo-Norman fortress, where history comes alive as you explore its towering walls, stunning views, and centuries-old stories.

The Hill Of Tara

Experience the mystical Hill of Tara, the ancient seat of Ireland’s High Kings, where breathtaking views and legendary tales of Irish heritage await.

New Town Monastry

Explore the serene ruins of the medieval Newtown Monastery in Trim, where ancient stonework and peaceful surroundings offer a glimpse into Ireland’s rich ecclesiastical history.
